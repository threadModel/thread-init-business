Privacy Policy
1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information
  • Name, email address, phone number, and company details when you inquire about our services.
  • Payment and billing information when you engage our services.
  • Information about your preferences, goals, and future requirements to better tailor our services and offerings.
b. Non-Personal Information
  • Log data, such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing behavior on our website.
  • Cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and gather analytics.

We use the information collected for the following purposes:
